Hello there! My name is Valerie Vasquez, and I’m the owner of Val'z yummy Bakeshop! As long as I can remember I have always been in the kitchen, whipping up something delicious. It’s been my dream since I was twelve to open up a bakery. This dream culminated after watching my Grandfather run his business and basically running a makeshift bakery from my small kitchen. In May of 2013 I had the privilege of graduating amidst being the first college graduate. I graduated with a Bachelors of Science from Fresno State. However, due to the pandemic I had the opportunity to reevaluate and realize, there’s no point in waiting to do what you’ve always dreamed of. After years of dreaming, hoping, planning, baking, I can safely say, Val’z Yummy Bakeshop is just that - my dream. I hope you enjoy!
